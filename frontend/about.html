Unreserved Ticketing System (UTS)

Issuing of UTS tickets:

  • Passengers can purchase Unreserved Ticket 3 days in advance (excluding the day of journey) for journeys above 200 km.
  • Unreserved tickets for any distance can be purchased on the same day.
  • Passengers can also purchase half-yearly (HST) and yearly (YST) season tickets.

How to book a Journey Ticket?

  • The passenger should have Android or Windows UTS mobile apps.
  • He/she must be a registered user in the Mobile Ticketing System.
  • The passenger should have sufficient money in their account.

Cancellation of UTS Tickets:

Unused tickets with no seat/berth reservation are eligible for cancellation with clerkage deduction, provided:

  • The ticket is presented within 3 hours of issue.
  • For advance tickets, cancellation is allowed up to 24:00 hours before the journey day.
  • Cancellation allowed only when terminals are online.
  • Cancellation is not permitted at JTBS counters.

Booking inside station premises:

Booking UTS mobile tickets inside station premises is not allowed as per Railway rules.
